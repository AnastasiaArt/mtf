MISSION

Medtechfund LLC is focusing on investing in a variety of medical projects. We are investing using different approaches including convertible loans and a direct participation in the capital of our portfolio companies. At the moment Medtechfund LLC has several companies in its portfolio, including a distributor of medical equipment and products, and a company working in transportation and distribution of medical drugs.

FIELDS FOR INVESTMENT

  • New nuclear medicine technologies;
  • New drugs development;
  • New diagnostic tools including technologies applicable for diagnosing new type of diseases (e.g. COVID-19);
  • Advanced medical imaging technologies;
  • Medical engineering;
  • Creation of a new generation of medical facilities.

Members

Medtechfund LLC. welcomes new qualified members who could invest their expertise, business connections and capital in developing our portfolio companies’ business. In return our members obtain shares in our portfolio companies and direct commission fees for helping facilitate deals of our portfolio companies.
